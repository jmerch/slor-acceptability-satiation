Instructions

In this experiment, you will be reading and rating some English sentences and answering some questions about their meaning. Your job is to rate how acceptable the sentence sounds, determine whether you understand it, and give your best rephrasing of the sentence.

When rating the questions, please use your intuition rather than school grammar.

For example, the sentence "Who did you go to school with?" is considered bad according to school grammar (because of the stranded preposition with). However, most English speakers find it totally acceptable. If you think a sentence is acceptable, you should give it a high rating regardless of what you learned in school.
